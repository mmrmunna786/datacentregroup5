Welcome to our Datacenter Designer  Web Application

This is a simple and intuitive web app where you can easily choose your desired location from the dropdown menu.

Then choose an estimated size for your datacenter from the dropdown menu.

Then input the amount of energy for your chosen datacenter size.

Finallly, you will get all the information about your datacenter.

For example, a small datacenter which requires 2 GWh of energy will yeald the following informaion:

"Your datacentre would build 2,667 m2 of solar farm.
Your datacentre would build 571 km2 of wind farms.
Your datacentre's cost for building renewables is: £790,476
After building renewables in the 1st year your rolling energy costs would be: £0
Your datacentre's initial CO₂ emissions: 61.00 metric tons.
Your datacentre's yearly CO₂ emissions: 0.00 metric tons."
